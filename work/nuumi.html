Case Study

Studio
Nuumi

Studio Nuumi wanted an online store that let their products do the talking. They offer beautiful hand-crafted furniture created from reclaimed materials and sourced from around their home in South Yorkshire. We designed a website that put their gorgeous products front and center and helped tell their story along the way.

Punctuated with impactful typography and an earthy colour pallete, we really wanted to reinforce the companies use of reclaimed materials.

Customers told us they were interested in the products and how they were made; we kept the navigation simple to allow them to explore the shop or the story.
